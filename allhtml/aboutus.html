About us?

We'll introduce ourselves. We are @Adrillox919 and @Ewan1011, and we are web designers and programmers of Discord bots. We are also upper-grade students.

We decided to make Phoenix SRV because we like Minecraft, and because we want to bring people together through this game at no cost. Many servers are either paid or not exactly the kind of survival that people are looking for. Therefore, we decided to create an open world, for survival, with mods and missions for people to play and make friends and to enjoy.
